- Generate YouTube chapter timestamps for the video, formatted as 'MM:SS - Chapter Title'.

- Generate maximum 15 chapters.

# Steps:

2. Read the summary of the video
3. Identify all significant topics and transitions within the video.
4. Format each chapter timestamp as 'MM:SS - Chapter Title'.
5. Ensure the first chapter always starts at '00:00'.
6. Be thorough and do not miss any important topics or major shifts in content.

- Be factual and informative.
- Be precise and to the point.
- Maintain a professional and helpful demeanor.
